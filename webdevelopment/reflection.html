Welcome! Thanks for checking out my reflection on my career.

So far in my career, I am very proud of how far I have come. I have created a strong foundation built on adaptability, taking on responsibilities, and a yearn for learning in how both technical and human systems work together. My experience in fast paced service environments taught me how to perform under great stress while still communicating clearly and maintaining attention to detail while multitasking. These skills have helped me shape my academic life in Information Technology and enforced the importance of teamwork and responsibilities in professional settings.

While pursuing my degree, I have been able to dive deep into both practical and technical skills. I have been able to translate my emphasis on teamwork in my career to my coursework, which is now evolving more into collaborative work like software engineering. In addition to collaborative work, I am still furthering my more practical skills through classes in cybersecurity, databases, and machine learning.

Outside of the classroom, I have been able to explore a variety of different projects both alike my career and a little more variety. Whether it be web development, 3D modelling, game development, or tinkering with hardware, these personal projects have all been great for my pursuit of learning outside just academics. Passions like cooking, baking, hiking, and crafts such as embroidery or origami assist me in having a more full life and give me passions outside my core interests for me to continue learning.

Finally, my goal is to build a career in IT where I can continue learning while making a meaningful impact. I am particularly interested in positions that allow me to continue problem solving with accessibility, collaboration, and creative outlets. As technology evolves at the rapid pace it has been, I hope to evolve my skills and knowledge alongside it, as well as contributing to thoughtful and accessible solutions for more people to be able to utilize technology. I also hope to work with assistive technologies and help users better understand the tools that can help them every day. Through this, I believe I will enjoy a career rich in fulfillment and passion.
